Problem Description

Writer’s block and artist’s block are conditions where people are unable to produce new work, either writing or art. It usually is a loss of creativity that people get into when they work for too long or too much. Many people that write or make art experience a creative block that leads to frustration at being unable to make anything. Even famous authors and artists face it and have given advice on what they do to overcome their blocks. Source

Most current solutions, according to a quick search, are articles with tips and advice and some paid apps that keep people focused on writing. There is a lack of resources that do something new to try and help people overcome their block.Some solutions that have been found have been done multiple times, and others involve solutions like limiting work time or walking around to get inspiration.This article lists some apps that can help people write, but a lot of them need to be bought.This website is just one of many possible random generator websites. It offers a lot of options but going through them seems to need a lot of input, which a user may not be able to provide There is an opportunity to create something that can help people get over creative blocks that are made specifically for that purpose and are accessible to people that may not be able to use other methods.

Proposed Application

A potential app that could attempt to alleviate the creative block on users is a website that allows users to post ideas, get help from a community, use a random plot/idea/character generator, or play a game. All features would be built with the purpose to help the user get over their creative block or it can act as a place where the user can spend time there when they need to rest from creating.

Potential users for this app are professional or amateur writers and artists. This could even be used by students that need to write papers. What the users will be able to do on the website is read and post to public forum, rest and play a text game in order for them to get ideas, and use the random generators to get random characters, plots, plot twists, and names.

This website would be a possible solution as it targets the suspected cause of creative blocks, which is a lack of imagination or creativity, overworking, or something else. by providing a place for users to spend their time recuperating.

User Walkthrough

Alex is an art student that wants to add something to his portfolio, but he feels overworked and stressed out from school assignments. Although he wants to create something, he is not satisfied with the things he’s sketched so he decides to take a break until he feels he can make something again. Alex stumbles upon a website, tentatively named Unblocker.

  1. He sees a link for the forum and clicks it
  2. He opens a thread and reads some tips that may help him
  3. He hovers over the navigational bar and sees a game that he can play
  4. He clicks the link to play. The game allows him to explore branching story paths with just clicks of a button
  5. He clicks the site logo and goes back to the home page after reaching an ending
  6. He sees a link to a character generator and clicks it
  7. He chooses to generate multiple descriptions and clicks a button to generate them
  8. He clicks the save button to save descriptions that he likes
