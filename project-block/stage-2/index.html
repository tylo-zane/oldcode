BLOCK

About

Block is a choose your own adventure game designed to help you, the player, with your artist's block. The game features a short story with 4 endings, and after reaching the end of a route, you will be rewarded with a prompt and a color palette.

The game can be played when you want inspiration for something to draw or you just want to have some fun. The color palette is generated by your choices and the object to draw is randomly generated.

How To Play

Playing Block only requires a mouse or a touchscreen in order to be able to select your choices.

Do I need an account to play?

No, an account is not necessary. Having an account saves your palettes and prompts, which you can access at the Your Prompts page.

Having an account will also save your progress if you leave mid-game.
